Maple Rum Flip

How to Make Maple Rum Flip

A classic flip shakes together liquor (or fortified wine) with sugar and a whole egg. This fall version uses rum, maple, rosemary, and nutmeg.

Ingrediens

  • 1 1/2 ounces aged rum (such as Appleton Estate®)
  • 1/2 ounce orange liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
  • 1/2 ounce pure maple syrup
  • 1 pasteurized whole egg
  • ice as needed
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • freshly grated fresh nutmeg, for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine rum, orange liqueur, maple syrup, and egg in a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake until pressure builds and mixture starts to emulsify, about 45 seconds. Add ice and rosemary to shaker. Cover and shake until outside of shaker is frosted, about 30 seconds. Strain into a glass. Garnish with nutmeg.
